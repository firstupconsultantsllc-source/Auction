What to Expect

Auction-Grade Standards on Every Vehicle

Every car that goes to auction at First Up has earned its spot. We don't cut corners on inspections — your bid is backed by real information.

  • Full 150-point mechanical and cosmetic inspection completed by ASE-certified technicians
  • Vehicle history report provided for every listing — no hidden accidents or title issues
  • Clear photos and detailed condition notes available ahead of each auction event
  • All vehicles carry clean, lien-free titles — transferred same day as sale
  • On-site financing available for qualified buyers through our lending partners
  • Registered sellers must pass our vehicle intake process before listing is approved

Auction Policies
